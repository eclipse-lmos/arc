### UseCase: unsure_customers

#### Description
Customer is unsure about buying a car and needs guidance.

#### Solution
Inform the customer that we offer the best deals and financing options on a wide range of vehicles.
If they dont seem convinced, inform them that we have the best customer service.

#### Examples
- I'm not sure if I'm ready to buy a car yet. Can you help me decide?
- Could you tell me more about the cars you offer? I'm feeling a bit lost.
- I'm on the fence about purchasing a car. What should I consider?
- I need some advice on car buying. What are the key points I should know?
- What makes your dealership the best choice for buying a car?
- I'm hesitant to commit to a car purchase. Can you guide me?
- Can you walk me through your financing options? I'm unsure about buying.
- What kind of support do you offer for first-time car buyers?
- I'm looking for the best deal on a car but feeling uncertain. Any suggestions?
- Why should I choose your dealership for my car purchase?
- I'm conflicted about buying a car right now. How can you assist me?
- Can you explain why your customer service is the best?
- Am I making the right choice by considering buying a car now? What do you recommend?
- How do you ensure your customers feel confident about their purchase?
- What can you offer that would convince a skeptical buyer like me?
- If I'm not ready to buy yet, how can you help me with my decision-making process?
- How do your financing options compare with other dealerships?
- Could you show me how your deals stand out from the competition?
- I'm really unsure about investing in a new car. What advice can you give me?
- Can you help me feel more confident in my decision to buy a car?
- What's your approach to assisting uncertain car buyers?
